TWICE

Twice is a south korean girl group which is formed by the survival show "Sixteen" wherein the trainees from the company will compete with each other through their talents in order for them to debut. The final line up/ Members are Nayeon (Lead Vocalist), Jihyo (Main Vocalist), Momo (Main Dancer), Mina (Lead Dancer), Sana (Lead Dancer), Chaeyoung (Main Rapper), Tzuyu (Main Visual). Their Company is "JYP Entertainment".Twice is also known not only in korea but internationally also. Currently, Twice's latest comeback was "Fancy". The fans of Twice are called "ONCEs"

MONSTA X

Monsta X is a south korean boy group. They were created through the survival program "NO MERCY". Monsta X debuted on May 14, 2015, under Starship Entertainment. Monsta X is also under the US label Maverick Agency as of February 26th 2019. The members are composed of Changkyun (Lead Rapper), Minhyuk (Vocalist), Jooheon (Main Rapper), Shownu (Main Dancer), Hyungwon (Lead Dancer), Wonho (Lead Dancer), Kihyun (Main Vocalist). Monsta X's latest comeback was "Alligator". Their company is "Starship Entertainment". Their fandom name is "monbebe"

BLACKPINK

Blackpink is a south korean girl group which is formed by the YG Entertainment. They debuted on August 8, 2016 with their digital single album "Square One". The group is composed of Lisa (Main Dancer), Rose (Main Vocalist), Jeni (Main Rapper), Jisoo (Main Visual). Blackpink is also known internationally and a kpop girl group who is a monster in terms of views in their music videos. The Fans of BlackPink are called "BLINKs".
